Game Rules

Use the arrow keys to move the snake.

Eat the red squares (apples) to grow.

Avoid colliding with yourself.

Points per apple - Without Walls:

  • Easy: 1 point
  • Medium: 3 points
  • Hard: 5 points

Points per apple - With Walls:

  • Easy: 3 point
  • Medium: 6 points
  • Hard: 10 points
